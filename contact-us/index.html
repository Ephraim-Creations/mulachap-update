Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Do I need to prepare any documents before contacting you?

For specific loan inquiries, having basic financial information ready is helpful, but no documents are needed for initial contact.

Is there a fee for the initial consultation?

No, our initial consultation is completely free. We'll discuss your needs and options without any obligation.

What areas do you serve?

We serve clients throughout Kenya, with our main office in Nairobi. We also offer virtual consultations for clients outside Nairobi.
