Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Mulachap Ventures is a consultancy firm that was created to help business and employed people to get the financing they need in an institution that offers better terms.

Its operations cover countrywide and offers solutions to any financial need.

Over the years, we've helped over 800+ clients secure financing totaling over KSh 100M in loans.

Meet Our Founder

Immaculate Wangari Kimondo

Founder & CEO

With over 10 years in the financial sector, Immaculate founded Mulachap Ventures to bridge the gap between financial complexity and client clarity by providing expert guidance, strategic planning and personalized solutions that drive sustainable success.

Her expertise in financial consulting has shaped our client-focused approach.

"We believe in creating financial solutions that align with our clients' long-term goals and aspirations."

Under her leadership, Mulachap Ventures has become a trusted name in financial consulting across Kenya.
